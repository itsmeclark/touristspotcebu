KAWASAN FALLS

Kawasan Falls is honestly one of the most beautiful waterfalls
we have ever seen The reason for that is mainly the incredibly
turquoise blue water.

TUMALOG FALLS

The sheer size of Tumalog Falls is incredible, and the many
paths of water make it a beautiful spot to relax in.

Magellan's Cross is a reminder of how Christianity was brought
to the Philippines. Christianity in the Philippines started in the
1500s when Spanish forces led by Ferdinand Magellan

MOALBOAL BEACH

Moalboal Beach features soft white sands and clear blue
waters, perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and relaxing by the
shore.

TAOIST TEMPLE

Taoists this is a place of solemnity and worship, while for
everyone else, it is a great way to understand the history and
importance of the Chinese culture.
